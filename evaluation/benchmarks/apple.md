## Business overview
Apple designs and sells consumer electronics (iPhone, Mac, iPad, Wearables)
and a fast-growing Services business (App Store, iCloud, Apple Music, AppleCare,
advertising, payments). iPhone remains the largest revenue driver; Services
carries the highest gross margin.
